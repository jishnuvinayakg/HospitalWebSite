Sreevalsam Ayurvedic Hospital

Sreevalsam ayurvedic hospital has over 20 years of excellance in the field of ayurvedic treatments. Strated by Dr KM Rajakumaran Nair who specializes mainly in spinal corrections due to Disc Prolapse, Disc Degeneration, Spondylosis( Lumbar & Cervical), Scolyosis, Loss of lordosis. Dr KM Rajakumaran Nair graduated from the renown Kottakal Ayurveda College.

Hospital is situated in the temple town of Erumely, in close proximity to the famous Sree Dharma Shastha Temple. Hopistal OP and Pharmacy are located in Erumely town and IP is located few kilometers away from town without any disturbances from town, making it an ideal place for treatment. Patients will have a homely atmoshphere.
